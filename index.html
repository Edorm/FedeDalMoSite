The Designer

Where Italian Tradition Meets Modern Elegance

Federica Dal Molin is an Italian fashion designer based in Dubai, specializing in made-to-measure luxury garments. Trained at Milan's Academy of Fine Arts of Brera, she brings a rigorous foundation in pattern-cutting, fabric innovation, and silhouette research to every piece she creates.

Her work has been showcased at Milan Fashion Week, recognized with the Lineapelle Young Designers Edition award, and featured in international fashion publications. With experience spanning renowned Italian fashion houses and bespoke ateliers, she has honed a distinctive approach: merging traditional craftsmanship with a contemporary, wearable sensibility.

Today, Federica works one-on-one with clients across Dubai, designing garments that are as unique as the women who wear them — from evening gowns and cocktail dresses to tailored separates for every occasion that matters.

How It Works

Your Garment, Your Journey

Every piece begins with a conversation. Federica comes to you — at your home, office, or a location of your choice — to ensure a seamless, personal experience from start to finish.

01
Consultation
A personal meeting to understand your style, occasion, and vision. Together you explore fabrics, silhouettes, and details.
02
Design
Federica develops sketches and selects materials tailored to your brief — from Italian silks to innovative textiles.
03
Fitting
Precise measurements and fittings at your location. Every seam, drape, and proportion is refined to your body.
04
Delivery
Your finished piece, crafted with care and delivered to you — ready for the moment it was made for.
